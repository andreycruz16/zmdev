Bespoke WordPress website built to order for Anne Higgins, and independent fashion designer
based in West London.

Anne came to me with a vision and very specific guidelines on how she wanted to portray her brand
online. I took her styleguide, photos, logo and digital copy to craft a custom WordPress theme
that matched her existing brand.
